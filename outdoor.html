Checkout some of the fun trails in Phoenix!

Hole in the Rock

Are you looking for kids friendly trail? Hole in the Rock is the perfect place to go! This easy level trail is a fun hiking introduction for your little one. It only takes minutes to reach the peak where you can see the breathtaking view through the hole in the rock. Although it is an easy trail, it also a perfect place for everyone to enjoy the Phoenix morning breeze while watching the sunrise.

Phoenix Mountains Preserve

Being one of the most popular spots for outdoor activity in Phoenix, the preserve offers biking, hiking, and beautiful views of the city. This medium-hard difficulty level trail is perfect for family outdoor activity.
