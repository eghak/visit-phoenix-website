Have fun with your family at some of the best attractions in Phoenix!

Phoenix Zoo

Enjoy seeing more than 1000 animals on display while walking on some different themed trails: Arizona Trail, Africa Trail, Tropics Trail, and Children's Trail. It's a great place for you to spend time together with your family and friends!

Enchanted Island Amusement Park

Looking for a place for your kids to have fun? Enchanted Island Amusement Park the right place to go! The park offers many fun rides for children (and adults too!), from carousel to roller coaster.
